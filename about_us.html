About us

Premium underground hoodies, handmade in Reus.

Rebellion in Cloth

REKOM was born from a simple truth: not everyone is meant to fit in. We design for the ones who walk against the current — the ones who choose identity over trends and meaning over noise.

Our universe is built on urban rebellion, exclusivity, and extreme quality. Nothing leaves our workshop unless it feels intentional, powerful, and unmistakably REKOM.

Premium Underground Aesthetic

Our visual language comes from the underground: raw energy, sharp silhouettes, and a bold attitude that doesn’t ask for permission.

Every hoodie is crafted to look and feel like more than clothing — a statement, a presence, a permanent extension of who you are.

Handmade in Reus

All REKOM pieces are handmade in Reus (Spain) in our personal workshop. No outsourcing. No shortcuts.

We work with premium materials, limited quantities, and full control over every step of the process. Because true exclusivity starts where mass production ends.

Every edition is limited. Every piece is intentional. Every garment is built to last — to stay with you for years, not seasons.

Built on Urban Culture

Our inspiration comes from the streets: the culture, the movement, the chaos, the authenticity.

REKOM exists for those who want a piece that grows with them — a companion in their story, not just another item in their wardrobe.

Direct. Raw. REKOM.

We don’t chase trends. We don’t compromise on quality. We don’t dilute our voice. REKOM is rebellion refined — bold, precise, and unapologetically ours.

This is not just clothing. It’s attitude. It’s identity. It’s REKOM™.
