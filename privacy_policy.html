Privacy Policy

How we collect, use and protect your personal data when you interact with REKOM.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how REKOM™ collects, uses, and protects the personal information provided by users when browsing or interacting with our website. We are firmly committed to safeguarding your data and ensuring transparent and responsible handling of your information.

By providing personal details through any form or interaction on our site, you acknowledge and accept the terms described in this document. Please note that this Privacy Policy may be updated over time, and we encourage you to review it periodically to remain informed.

2. Information We Collect

REKOM™ may collect personal information, including but not limited to:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Contact details
  • Demographic information
  • Shipping or billing data when necessary for completing an order

Additional information may be requested when required to process purchases, deliver orders, issue invoices, or provide customer support.

3. Use of Collected Information

The information collected on our website is used to:

  • Provide a better browsing and purchasing experience
  • Maintain customer and order records
  • Improve our products and services
  • Send updates, special offers, new product releases, or relevant marketing content

Any promotional emails will be sent to the address provided by the user, and you may unsubscribe at any time.

REKOM™ is committed to ensuring your data remains secure. We use modern, continuously updated security systems to prevent unauthorized access.

4. Cookies

Cookies are small files stored on your device to enhance your browsing experience. They allow websites to identify user preferences, analyze traffic, and deliver more personalized content.

REKOM™ uses cookies for:

  • Tracking frequently visited pages
  • Statistical analysis
  • Improving site functionality

Cookies do not grant access to your computer or personal data unless you choose to provide it.

You may accept or decline cookies at any time. Most browsers accept cookies automatically, but you can modify your settings to disable them. If cookies are disabled, certain features of our website may not function correctly.

5. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party sites of potential interest. Once you leave our site by clicking such a link, REKOM™ has no control over the destination website and is not responsible for their privacy policies, terms of use, or the protection of your personal data.

We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of any external sites you access.

6. Control of Your Personal Information

You may request limitations on the collection or use of your personal data at any time.

Whenever you are asked to fill out a form (such as account creation, newsletter subscription, or purchase information), you may opt in or out of receiving marketing communications. If you previously subscribed to our newsletter or promotional emails, you may unsubscribe at any time.

REKOM™ does not sell, share, or distribute personal information without your consent, except where legally required by a court order.

7. Policy Updates

REKOM™ reserves the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes will take effect immediately upon publication on our website.
